## **Nature and Purpose**

- **Definition:** A maс╣Еgala┼Ыloka is a benedictory verse, often composed as an encomium (praise) or invocation, addressed to a deity, guru, or patron[7].

- **Placement:** Traditionally appears on the title page or as the header of the manuscript, and sometimes at the end as well[7].

## **Development and Historicity**

- **Ancient Roots:** The practice traces back to Vedic times, where auspicious mantras (*maс╣Еgala mantras*) were recited at the beginning of rituals for success and peace[1].
